About Me

I am Alessandro Sciarra, a physicist and software engineer working at the intersection of OSINT, data visualization, and modular system design. I build tools, platforms, and workflows that emphasize clarity, reproducibility, and privacy-aware engineering.

My Background

I hold a Ph.D. in Physics and have worked across research, imaging engineering, and software development. My work spans OSINT platforms, interactive maps, offline-first applications, and technical architectures designed for transparency and scalability.

I enjoy designing modular repositories, building reproducible workflows, and creating tools that help people explore complex information in intuitive ways. When I’m not developing new systems, I explore emerging technologies, refine my technical workflows, or work on long-term research projects.

Data & Visualization
